About Us Page

This platform is helping manage the delivered packages in campus, it has eliminated the paper wastage as everything is managed digitally here. The system uses OTP mechanism to authenticate each student and hence prevent misplacement of packages.

Lost and Found Management module is enabling the user to click a photo of the found item or in case of lost item and share the info with their peers. The lost/found items are listed on the web page category wise which the owner can easily search and can collect from the given location.
